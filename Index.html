About

Qingdao is located on the peninsula of Shangdong Province, east China. It is an economy center and important tourism attraction in the province, which is well populated with the number of permanent inhabitants of 9, 29 million.

Qingdao is considered to be a big city in China with the land scale of 11282 km2. It is consisted of 7 districts and three satellite towns. It is neighber to Rizhao, Weifang, and Yantai. Korean peninsula is across the Yellow Sea.

The economy in Qingdao is constantly growing in the past few years in syncro pace of the bigger picture of China. In 2107 the GDP is 1103.7 billion yuan. Qingdao is famous for its electrical devices and beer industry in terms of economy.

POPULAR ATTRACTIONS

Qingdao is a coastal city with mountains. The unique landscape made the city a perfect spot for outdoor activities such as sailing and hiking. Meanwhile the city was occupied by German troops for a period during the history, which has left the city some multi-cultural vibes in the atmosphere.

NATURE

Lao Mountain is the most significant nature landmark in Qingdao. It extends all the way along the coast line. There is a typical view of nothern China forest on the mountain, where you can see a lot of big stones and pine trees. There is also a national famous Taoism temple on one of the highest tops of Lao Mountain.

CULTURE

The city has learned beer brewing technich from a Danish scientist during the German occupation period. The factoty then developed into China's most famous beer brand "Tsingtao Beer". In Qingdao beer is a very prominent culture feature, it is a very important in the everyday city life. Almost all residents of the city love beer, and they enjoy drinking daily freshly-crafted beer on the street. Of course they refuse to drink beer of other brands in China.
